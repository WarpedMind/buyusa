Thank you for supporting BuyUSA.support by adding your company’s brand(s) and product(s) info.

How to Start

1. Create A Brand

In order to add any products, there must be at least one brand created. If your company and your brand are one and the same, please create a new brand by clicking the “Add Brand” button below or in the main navigation and then fill out the info in the form with the same info listed in your company profile. If your brand is separate and unique from your standard company info, please create that brand, also by clicking that button. Perhaps your company owns multiple brands in multiple verticals. Feel free to add these brands as well by repeating this process. Also be sure to employ keywords that sufficiently describe your product so that people searching BuyUSA.support can easily find your particular product.

NOTE: The brand you are adding is NOT visible to the public until you select “Published” in the Publish field. If you with to remove a brand that is visible to the public, change the selection to “Unpublished”.

2. Create A Product

Once you have added a brand, you may now add a product. Click on the “Add Product” button below or in the main navigation and then fill out the form. Be sure to select what brand this product is sold or manufactured under. Also be sure to employ keywords that sufficiently describe your product so that people searching BuyUSA.support can easily find your particular product.

NOTE: The product you are adding is NOT visible to the public until you select “Published” in the Publish field. If you wish to remove a product that is visible to the public, change the selection to “Unpublished”.

3. Repeat

Feel free to add multiple brands or products where desired appropriate.

4. Edit A Brand or Product

In order to edit an existing brand or product, click on “My Brands/Products” to view the list of the brands and products you have created. You’ll be able to edit your preferred brand or product there.
