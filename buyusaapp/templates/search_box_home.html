THIS IS AN INFORMATION-ONLY SITE INTENDED TO INFORM THE PUBLIC OF PRODUCTS THAT ARE MADE OR ASSEMBLED IN THE USA, AS WELL AS WHERE AND/OR HOW THESE ITEMS MAY BE PURCHASED.

We are a donation-only help site supporting our U.S. manufacturers, workers, economy, and country by listing over 384,200 actual U.S. based manufacturers and 1.2 billion products, as well as where these retail, wholesale, manufacturer to manufacture, and everyday items may be purchased. Through our TV commercials, social media, and your help, we will put as much accurate information out as possible, so that the public can make a choice to buy U.S. made products or not.

If you type in an item and no item comes up, unfortunately, that product is either currently not manufactured or assembled in the U.S. or the manufacturer has not listed their products with us yet. If any information on this site is inaccurate, PLEASE email us so we can verify and correct our information. If you are a manufacturer, big or small, that produces products in the U.S. or sells products and you are not listed on this site, please register here.
